WHO IS MONKEY'S CYMBAL?

ABOUT ME

Monkey's Cymbal is Pablo Martin, a sound designer, music composer and mixing engineer with years of experience in music production.

You can find here all you need for your videogame audio, from the very first audio concepts to original composition and sound recordings, as well as audio programing and implementation to have everything working smoothly.

The main goal is to create an audio experience that completes and makes your videogame even bigger!

AUDIO REEL

WHAT DO I DO?

Audio Design

I'm a firm supporter of the idea that videogames have to be considered a form of art, and that every one of them have something to say artistically an esthetically.

My most important task is to help you find what your game have to say, and give it a voice to sing it loudly.

Music Composition

Although I'm specialized in electronic music production, I've worked for all kind of projects, creating everything from orchestral mockups to fully synthetised 80s music.

The key factor here is to be flexible, and I'm a huge melomaniac with an intense knowledge about music, synthesis and different music genres

Assets

Sound assets are the main components for the sound design, and every one of them requires special atention to fit the game intention and enable the player immersion.

Each of them need to have an specific meaning and purpose within the game, whether it gives feedback to the player or triggers an emotional response.

Mixing and Mastering

I've been mixing and mastering lots of music for the last few years, so I can asure you that gear limitations are no longer an excuse to make your sounds assets and your music shine.

Mixing and mastering are crucial to give your audio a polished and hi-fi character, so your videogame can sound as big and professional as you want.

Cinematics

Game cinematics are often undervaluated, but they are a key component for the narrative and help building the emotional journey.

Linear audio for trailers can also be quite informative and here we can help to create impact, whether it means blasting taiko drums or creating alien low-pitched drones

Implementation

Here we get to the hard work. Everything we did until now can be ruined if we don't manage to speak the same language.

We can help you bridge the gap between programming and audio production, from audio editing to audio imnplementation with software like FMOD and Wwise.
